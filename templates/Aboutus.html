About Alpha One

Alpha One Energi is a leading company specializing in the trade and management of liquid and solid waste, transforming them into renewable fuel sources. Our expertise encompasses a wide range of materials, including used cooking oil, used engine oil, and various derivatives of palm oil. We are committed to sustainability and innovation, ensuring that waste products are efficiently repurposed into valuable energy resources. By leveraging advanced technologies and eco-friendly practices, Alpha One Energi plays a crucial role in reducing environmental impact and promoting a greener future.

Vision

Our vision is to reduce carbon emissions by using biodiesel instead of fossil fuels, enhance the well-being of surrounding communities, and boost the circular economy in Indonesia. We strive to lead the world in adopting more sustainable fuel sources, combatting the use of used cooking oil for consumption. By championing these goals, we aim to create a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable environment for future generations.

Mission

Our mission are transforming used cooking oil into biodiesel, thus contributing significantly to the circular economy by purchasing waste. We are dedicated to employing local communities and educating them about the importance of proper waste disposal. Through these efforts, we aim to provide positive impacts on both society and the environment, fostering a culture of sustainability and responsibility.

Sustainability

We are committed to building a circular economy for Indonesia, ensuring resources are reused and recycled to minimize waste and maximize sustainability.
