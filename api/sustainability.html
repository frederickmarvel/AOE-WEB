Sustainability

Our mission of providing positive influence

For Our Environment

Collecting used cooking oil has significant benefits for the environment, one of which is preventing soil and ocean pollution. Improper disposal of used cooking oil can seep into the soil, contaminating groundwater, or flow into the ocean, damaging marine ecosystems. Additionally, used cooking oil can be processed into biodiesel, a renewable energy source that can reduce carbon emissions. By recycling used cooking oil into biodiesel, we can decrease our reliance on fossil fuels and help mitigate the impact of climate change.

For Our Community

Collecting used cooking oil also benefits communities by enhancing the circular economy in Indonesia. By collecting and recycling used cooking oil, we can reduce household waste and add value to materials previously considered waste. This process not only supports sustainability but also creates new job opportunities in the recycling and biodiesel production industries. Therefore, the collection of used cooking oil can contribute to the improvement of local economic welfare.

For Our Well-being

From a health perspective, collecting and disposing of used cooking oil properly can reduce the risk of repeatedly using cooking oil. Used cooking oil contains polar compounds that are toxic and can cause serious diseases, such as cancer and genetic mutations. By ensuring that used cooking oil is collected and processed correctly, we can prevent exposure to these harmful substances and protect public health from the potential dangers of repeatedly using used cooking oil.
